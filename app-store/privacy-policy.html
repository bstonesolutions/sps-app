Privacy Policy

SPS Way · Effective June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stone Property Solutions LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the SPS Way mobile application and web app (the "App"). The App is provided to our service customers and our field staff. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who uses this app

SPS Way serves two groups: customers of Stone Property Solutions, who use it to view their service history, invoices, and photos and to request service; and our staff, who use it to manage routes, complete service visits, and run day-to-day operations. Accounts are created and provisioned by us — there is no public sign-up.

We do not collect or store payment card numbers. Card and online payments are processed by QuickBooks (Intuit) through their secure checkout; we receive only whether an invoice was paid.

3. How we use information

  • To schedule, perform, document, and bill for services
  • To notify customers when a technician is on the way, has arrived, or has completed a visit
  • To respond to service requests and messages
  • To operate, secure, and improve the App

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers that help us run the App, and only as needed to provide it:

5. Data retention

We keep information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide services, comply with our legal and tax obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

6. Security

Access to app data requires authentication, and data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored with our hosting providers' security protections. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

7. Children's privacy

SPS Way is intended for business use and is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

8. Your choices & rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at the address below. Staff location sharing is active only while the app is in use during a job; you can disable location permission in your device settings, though some features will not work without it. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the "Effective" date above when we do, and material changes will be reflected in the App.
